HOW TO PLAY

Welcome to The Dungeon, an extensive underground labyrinth that you have been tasked to navigate by candlelight. To escape this derelict maze, your mission is to find the exit ladder that will take you back to the surface, and to civilization.


Follow these instructions below to find your way through the dark:

  • Objective: find the ladder (green tile) to escape the maze.

  • White tiles are viable floor tiles. Gray tiles are boundaries / walls within the dungeon through which you cannot pass.

  • Move your player through the dungeon with the arrow keys or with the W, A, S, D keys on your keyboard.

  • Your score is directly tied to the number of moves that you take on the board. Your score decreases with each move that you take. Finding the ladder in fewer moves leaves you with a higher score.

  • If your score hits zero before you find the ladder, the game is over.

  • Secret gems (purple tiles) exist on the board. Collecting them boosts your score by 500 points.

  • Hidden pits also exist on the board. These tiles look exactly like viable room tiles, but if you step on them, you will be transported back to your original starting point in the maze.

  • Easy, medium, and hard difficulties correspond to the map size, number of pits, the visible area around your player, and the total number of points alloted to you at the beginning of the game. The higher the difficulty, the larger the map size, the larger the quantity of pits, the smaller the visible area around your player, and the greater the number of points alloted to you at the start of the game.
